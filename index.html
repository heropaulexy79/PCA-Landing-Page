Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide world-class physical therapy/rehabilitation, home/elder care and physiotherapy training services throughout West and Central Africa — filling this critical gap in the healthcare ecosystem to improve health outcomes on the continent.

Our Vision

To be Nigeria’s leading physiotherapy/rehabilitation and home/elder care company by 2025.
